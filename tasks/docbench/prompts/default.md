# Document QA Task

You are given a PDF document (`document.pdf`) in your workspace. Your task is to answer a question about its contents.

## Instructions

1. Read `document.pdf` — it is a research paper or technical document.
2. Locate the relevant information needed to answer the question.
3. Write your final answer to `answer.txt`. Be concise and direct — just the answer, no preamble.

## Rules

- Base your answer strictly on what is in the document. Do not guess or fabricate.
- For numerical answers, include units if applicable.
- For multiple-choice or categorical answers, state just the value.
- Your `answer.txt` must contain only the answer and nothing else.
